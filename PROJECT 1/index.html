Bhuvneshwar Kumar

The Swing King

Bhuvneshwar Kumar is an Indian cricketer renowned for his exceptional swing bowling and ability to trouble batsmen with his precision and control. Born on February 5, 1990, in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, he made his debut for the Indian national team in 2012. Kumar quickly established himself as a vital member of the team, particularly in limited-overs formats. Known for his ability to swing the ball both ways, he is effective in both the early and death overs of an innings. His consistent performances have earned him accolades and recognition in international cricket. Additionally, Kumar has been a key player for Sunrisers Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League (IPL), contributing significantly to their success over the years. Despite facing injury setbacks, his resilience and dedication continue to make him a prominent figure in Indian cricket.
